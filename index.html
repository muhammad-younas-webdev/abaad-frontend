Our Story & Vision

Founded by national leaders and sector experts to be “a strategic driver for empowering community development in the mining sector—one of the key pillars of Saudi Vision 2030.”

Vision

To be the leading national entity in empowering local communities affected by mining activities.

Mission

Enable the mining sector to achieve sustainable development by delivering innovative, integrated solutions that reflect community needs and strengthen CSR practices.
